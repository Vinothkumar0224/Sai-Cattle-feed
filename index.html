Shri Sai Cattle Feed

“Cattle’s Strength, Farmer’s Prosperity — Shri Sai Cattle Feed.”

Founded in 2023, Shri Sai Cattle Feed was established with a single vision to provide farmers with high-quality, natural, and nutrient-rich cattle feed that ensures the strength and productivity of their cattle.

We understand that healthy cattle are the backbone of every successful dairy farmer. That’s why we source only the best ingredients — including corn, pulses, oil cakes, minerals, and salts — to produce feed that promotes better milk yield, stronger growth, and improved digestion.

Our dedication to purity, consistency, and farmer satisfaction has made us a trusted name among dairy farmers.
